XS Module
The XS expansion module allows Power Band to create kinetic force or energy attacks.
This allows powers such as flight, telekinesis or fire blasts.
Even without an XS chip, the XS module enhances strength and speed.

Price: $600  



XS Power Chips
XS Power List:
XS Blade: Create force blades.
XS Blast: Shoot force blasts.
XS Flight: Fly on force platforms.
XS Hook: Create a grappling hook.
XS Lift: Move distant objects.
XS Shield: Create force shields.
XS Flames: Shoot fire.
XS Frost: Shoot ice.
XS Shock: Shoot lightning.
XS Sonic: Shoot sound blasts.

To unleash the true power of the XS module, you will need an XS Power Chip.



Price: $200 each
