TM Module
The TM expansion module allows Power Band to create blasts of energy, such as fire
or lightning, with just a flick of the wrist.
Even without an TM chip, the TM module keeps the user at a stable temperature.

Price: $650  



TM Power Chips
TM Power List:
TM Aura: Warm/cool the area.
TM Flames: Shoot fire.
TM Frost: Shoot ice.
TM Shock: Shoot lightning.
TM Sonic: Shoot sound blasts.

To unleash the true power of the TM module, you will need an TM Power Chip.



Price: $200 each
