Extension Unit
A Power Band can only hold 5 Power Chips at once. But sometimes you need more.
For those days, the Extension Unit's got your back.
Once you sync it with your Power Band, it allows you to use an extra 10 Power Chips!
Still not enough? A single Power Band can connect to any number of Extension Units at once.

Price: $60  


Overcharger
Even powers aren't always enough and you have to kick it up to the next level.
Sometimes, you need to Overcharge.
Once synced with a Power Band, the Overcharger unit can be set up to boost
the power of a single standard Power Chip to before unseen levels.
 
Price: $950  


FX Overcharger
The stock Overcharger can only boost default chips.
But that might not be what you need; you may need a different power.
The FX Overcharger is, once set up, capable of boosting an XS Power Chip.
The FX Overcharger can not boost normal chips.

Price: $1100  


If you need even more power, you may wish to take a look at the Omega Overcharger.
