Costume Capsule
Being a superhero can be tricky... if you're trying to hide it, anyway. Costume Capsules can help.
Press the button on top and boom; your costume puts itself on.
When you're done, a quick move and it puts itself back away and no one knows.

Costume Customization
Every hero is different and so is their costume. A hero's look tells you a lot about who they are. When ordering a Costume Capsule, you can choose the color of each part of the costume and the symbol on the torso.

Cape:
For those with a certain flair, a cape can be added onto your costume. As with the rest of the suit, you choose the color; a  custom symbol can also be added to the cape. Price: +$10


Armored Costume:
Costumes can be good for more than looks; it can keep you safe from attack. If you need some extra protection from your foes, you can add a Kevlar weave to your costume as an added layer of defense. Price: +$100

Titan Armor:
If you need even greater protection from your foes, Titan Armor adds an array of metal plates to your costume. The Kevlar weave of the normal armor is provided at no additional charge. Price: +$750
