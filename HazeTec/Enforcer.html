Enforcer Band
The Enforcer Band is a special Power Band that comes pre-loaded with the Speed, Stamina and Strength powers.
Enforcer Bands do not support additional Power Chips.
Only law enforcement personal are permitted to order Enforcer Bands.

Price: $300 



XS Enforcer Band
The XS Enforcer Band is an upgraded Enforcer Band with a built in XS Module.
It is preloaded with the XS Flight power in addition to Speed, Stamina and Strength.

Price: $550 




TM Enforcer Band
The TM Enforcer Band is an upgraded Enforcer Band with a built in TM Module.
It is preloaded with the TM Sonic power in addition to Speed, Stamina and Strength.

Price: $550
